PILAU RICE

Description

Pilau is a traditional, beautiful fragrant rice dish made with many aromatic spices that adds an amazing depth of flavor to the rice. It can be made with either beef or chicken.

Ingredients

  • Basmati Rice
  • Pilau Masala
  • Crushed garlic
  • Beef
  • Crushed ginger
  • Bay Leaves
  • Cooking oil
  • Tomato paste
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Prepare the Ingredients to avoid wasting time
  2. Fry onion till golden brown
  3. Add garlic and ginger and fry. But don't let it burn
  4. Add the whole Pilau masala and cook till the aroma fills the house
  5. Add your pre-boiled beef and fry till golden brown
  6. Add rice and salt and mix well. Then add boiling wateer and let it cook till the water has reduced.
  7. Reduce heat then turn the Pilau and cover tightly. Let it cook perfectly.
  8. Serve with kachumbari
